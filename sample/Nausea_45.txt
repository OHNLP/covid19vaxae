Patient received Johnson & Johnson 0.5ml  Covid vaccine IM to left deltoid. While in observation area, patient reported nausea and "tingling throat." BP 170/120, HR 98. Patient began vomiting, observed to be scratching arms. She reported feeling itchy everywhere. 911 called. Patient reported tongue "felt funny" and throat felt tight. Epinephrine 0.3mg administered IM to left thigh at 10:50am.  Patient reported almost immediate relief in symptoms after receiving Epinephrine. She was transported to local ED via EMS.
