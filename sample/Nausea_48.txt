Patient c/o severe nausea. RN offered pt a seat near the bathroom. Pt sat down and waited until nausea decreased. RN gave pt a bottle of water and she drank about 60ml. After about 10 minutes, pt stated she was still nauseous but felt slightly better. RN ensured pt that she could stay as long as she needed to and until her symptoms subsided. Pt and family member agreed that they were comfortable enough to head home. RN instructed pt to notify healthcare worker of her episode of nausea before she received the second dose of the covid-19 vaccine.
