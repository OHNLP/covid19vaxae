Patient received first dose of Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine EUA on Jan 7, 2021.  She experienced some injection site pain the following day.  Seven days post-vaccine (1/14/21), patient noticed injection site swelling, area was very hot and mildly painful to the touch.  Patient took a Benadryl 25 mg and iced the area.  The following day the swelling resolved and only noticed a red mark that was left.  The following day, symptoms were mostly resolved.
